YRM2016

The conference

The ETSF YRM 2016 will be held at the Strand Campus of King’s College London (KCL) in London, UK. The campus is situated at the very centre of London 20 min walk from such tourist attractions as the Houses of Parliament, St Paul's Cathedral, Trafalgar Square, etc. It's easily accessibly by city transport such as tube or buses.
